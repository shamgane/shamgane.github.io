Biosequencing

Embedding the biosequences of MERS, SARS and SARS-CoV-2 using Global Vectors and Fasttext and classifying them using CNNs and LSTMs to capture peculiar characteristics of each. This research work was published in ICACDS and would be extended into a journal publication.

Converting the malware bytes into images and exploring the use of Residual Attention Networks to focus on the malignant sections and clasify accordingly. This research was published in ICCE and can be found here.
